Mao Zedong

Once a Librarian, Mao Zedong became head of the Chinese Communist Party and was allied with the KuoMinTang until 1927, when the KMT turned on the CCP. Due to a battle on both fronts, with the CCP and the Japanese, the KMT had time to grow their power. After a civil war between the the KMT and CCP, the Communists won and the People's Repiblic of China was founded in 1949.
